# Hypothesis Generation Checklist

Use this checklist when the user needs new possible explanations or wants to inspect a rich dataset without prematurely locking onto one hypothesis.

## Exploration Pass

- What background knowledge or prior result started the question?
- What views, plots, summaries, comparisons, or negative cases have not yet been inspected?
- What unexpected patterns appeared?
- Which patterns are robust enough to deserve follow-up?
- Which patterns are still exploratory and should not be treated as conclusions?

## Contradiction Pass

- What observation conflicts with expectation?
- What hidden assumptions could be false?
- What alternative explanations fit the observation?
- What prediction distinguishes the alternatives?
- What check would make the contradiction actionable?

## Translation Pass

- What metaphor, analogy, or story is guiding intuition?
- What variables, mechanisms, states, or traces would make it testable?
- What would the story predict if it were wrong?
- What is the first day-science test after the night-science idea?

## Conversation Pass

- Who is a trusted partner for a low-stakes idea-building conversation?
- What can be improved by "yes, and" before critique?
- What assumptions should be surfaced before selection?
- When should the idea move from protected exploration to rigorous testing?
