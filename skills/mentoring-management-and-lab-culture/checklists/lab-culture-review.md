# Lab Culture Review Checklist

Use this for a quick scan before deeper routing.

## Expectations

- Are meeting cadence, communication norms, authorship, data/code, feedback, working hours, time off, AI use, and help routes written down?
- Are expectations role-specific where roles differ?
- Is there a review cadence for expectations?

## Power And Safety

- Which relationships involve dependency for funding, authorship, recommendation letters, visas, access, or evaluation?
- Is there more than one advice or escalation route?
- Are privacy limits and retaliation concerns handled honestly?

## Participation And Belonging

- Who speaks, decides, receives opportunity, and gets credit?
- Are meetings designed for participation, dissent, and repair?
- Are missing perspectives treated as scientific blind spots?

## Workload And Boundaries

- Are long hours or constant availability being rewarded?
- Are flexibility, vacations, after-hours communication, and deadline exceptions explicit?
- Is workload reviewed before performance concerns are personalized?

## Feedback And Conflict

- Was the relevant expectation explicit before the problem occurred?
- Is the proposed feedback claim-focused, evidence-based, and actionable?
- Is the issue interpersonal, structural, or both?
- Are support, follow-up, and escalation triggers included?

## Follow-Up

- What will change?
- Who owns the change?
- When will it be reviewed?
- What would trigger escalation or revision?
