# Mentoring Expectations Checklist

## Relationship Setup

- Role, career stage, funding or evaluation dependencies.
- Meeting cadence, agenda owner, cancellation norms.
- Communication channels, response time, emergency route, after-hours boundaries.
- Project ownership, decision rights, documentation expectations.
- Feedback format, timing, and response process.
- Authorship, credit, revision expectations, and dispute route.
- Development goals, skills, introductions, leadership opportunities.
- Support routes beyond the primary mentor.
- Review cadence and update triggers.

## Power-Aware Checks

- Does the trainee have a way to raise concerns outside the PI relationship?
- Are expectations specific enough to be reviewed later?
- Are career goals allowed to diverge from the PI's path?
- Does hard feedback include support and next steps?
- Is the document treated as revisable, not as a one-time contract?
