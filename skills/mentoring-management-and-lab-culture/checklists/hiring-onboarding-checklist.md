# Hiring And Onboarding Checklist

## Before Hiring

- Define the role purpose and expected independence.
- List required skills and trainable skills.
- Identify supervision load and mentoring capacity.
- Write structured interview questions.
- Define evaluation criteria before reviewing candidates.
- Check funding runway, equipment, administrative support, PI time, and project readiness.
- Decide what onboarding will need in the first week, month, and quarter.

## Candidate Evaluation

- Use evidence tied to the rubric.
- Do not substitute prestige, visibility, or institution names for contribution.
- Check collaboration needs and complementary skills.
- Record concerns and support needs explicitly.

## Onboarding

- Provide the handbook and role-specific expectations.
- Assign first contacts and recurring meetings.
- Clarify communication, authorship, data/code, AI, safety, and help routes.
- Set a first project with realistic scope.
- Schedule first-month and first-quarter fit reviews.
- Ask what remains unclear after onboarding.
