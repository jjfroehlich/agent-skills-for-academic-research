# Management Templates

## IDP / Yearly Planning Agenda

- Progress and wins.
- Strengths to build on.
- Growth areas.
- Project and skill goals.
- Career direction and options.
- Support, introductions, and resources.
- Feedback to the mentor.
- Lab-dynamic repair or expectation reset.
- Timeline, owners, and next review.

## Collaboration Kickoff

- Shared question and success criteria.
- Each person's interests and constraints.
- Required expertise and when it enters the project.
- Decision rights and handoff points.
- Data, code, documentation, and storage norms.
- Authorship, credit, and public communication.
- Meeting cadence and conflict route.

## State-Of-Lab Review

- Funding and resource picture.
- Staffing and supervision capacity.
- Project portfolio: continue, pause, stop, or hand off.
- Publication and collaboration pressure points.
- Career-development needs.
- Meeting, handbook, or policy updates.
- Decisions, owners, and review dates.

## Handbook Section Template

- Purpose.
- Default expectation.
- Role-specific notes.
- What to do when the default does not fit.
- Who can help.
- Escalation path.
- Review cadence.

## Failure Triage Template

- What happened?
- Which type of failure is this: execution, design, measurement, hypothesis, valid negative result, inconclusive evidence, expectation mismatch, or useful boundary condition?
- What was learned?
- What remains valuable?
- Next decision: repeat, redesign, pivot, publish, archive, or stop.
