# Conversation Scripts

## Unclear Expectation Feedback

"I realize we have not made the deadline standard explicit. Let's reset it together. The pattern I have seen is ____. What constraints or misunderstandings should I know about? Going forward, the expectation is ____. I will provide ____. Let's check back on ____."

Use when a trainee missed deadlines, meetings, documentation, authorship duties, or communication norms that were implicit.

## Claim-Focused Criticism

"The concern is with the claim that ____. The evidence currently supports ____, but not ____. The assumption I want you to test is ____. A stronger next version would ____."

Use when feedback must be rigorous without becoming personal.

## Distress-Support Conversation

"I have noticed ____. I am concerned about you, not only the work. What support would be useful right now? We can discuss workload or timeline changes, and I also want to connect you with qualified confidential resources. Here is what I can and cannot keep private in my role."

Use only for non-clinical support. Route to qualified help for clinical, safety, harassment, retaliation, or emergency concerns.

## Repair After Reactive Feedback

"I want to revisit how I handled our last conversation. My feedback should have been clearer and less reactive. The substantive issue is ____. What impact did the conversation have, and what would make our next feedback discussion more useful?"

Use when the mentor's delivery damaged trust but the work issue still needs attention.

## Co-Mentoring Route

"This issue would benefit from advice beyond me. Let's identify one or two people who can provide independent guidance on ____. We will clarify what they are advising on, what stays between you and them, and what we need to coordinate together."

Use when the PI lacks expertise, has a conflict of interest, or the trainee needs independent advice.
